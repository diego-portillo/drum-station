About our

Drum Station

Drums Station is a Paraguayan Company specialized in custom hadmade drum building. We have 20 years of experience working for the most demanding drummers in terms of sound quality and gear durability. Our CEO Sebastian Gomez invites you come in person to test our products in our Asuncion located workshop.
